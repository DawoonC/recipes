Ingredients:
 - 1 can baked beans
 - 1 chopped onion
 - 3 slices of bacon
 - 3 chopped garlic
 - black pepper
 - basil
 - olive oil
 - chilli pepper

Directions:
 1. Fry chopped onions and garlics on the pan with olive oil.
 
 2. Add bacon into the pan and fry more.
 
 3. Pour baked beans and chilli into the pan, and cook more.

 4. When it's nicely cooked, serve with black pepper and basil.
